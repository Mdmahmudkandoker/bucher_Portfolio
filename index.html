My Project

My Project
  • The completed "3-machines-1-it" project presents the successful implementation of the "Studis4Studis" platform. This is an online platform for uncomplicated exchange among BFH students, including a classifieds platform for school supplies and tutoring opportunities. I am particularly proud to have developed the backend for this application, using Strapi as the central data management system. The application is at an advanced stage, with features already implemented such as navigation, login and the display of classifieds. In addition to my contribution on the backend, the project focuses on improving the user experience by integrating Angular Material and ngx LightBox. Looking to the future, a possible extension to a BFH forum is planned to further promote student exchange.
